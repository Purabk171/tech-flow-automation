Shift-Left Compliance Engine

Compliance in Tech Flow is not an afterthought; it is embedded deeply into the generation loop. Break down the multi-pass validation logic used by the Compliance Review Agent (CRA).

◧ 1. Brand Voice Pass

Checks tone, style, and structure against vectorized brand guidelines. Outputs soft warnings for deviations.

◨ 2. Terminology Pass

Exact-match checks against the enterprise glossary. Employs a 'Linting' approach during drafting to catch errors early.

▩ 3. Regulatory Pass

Evaluates claims against hard legal rules (e.g., SEC, HIPAA). Results in hard blocks forcing auto-rewrites or developer queue routing.
